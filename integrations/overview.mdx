## Integration Categories

### Marketing Automation Platforms

Connect your marketing automation platform to export emails directly from Migma. Our integrations support merge tags, API-based exports, and automatic field mapping.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Klaviyo" icon="k" href="/integrations/klaviyo">
    **One-click export** with Liquid variables preserved
    - Export campaigns and templates
    - Automatic list sync
    - Flow integration support
    - Merge tags preserved
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mailchimp" icon="mailchimp" href="/integrations/mailchimp">
    **Direct campaign push** with merge tags
    - Create campaigns instantly
    - Template library export
    - Audience synchronization
    - Merge tag support
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Email Service Providers (ESPs)

Send emails directly through your preferred ESP or use Migma's built-in sending infrastructure.

<Card title="Email Service Providers" icon="server" href="/integrations/email-service-providers">
  **Support for multiple ESPs:**
  - Amazon SES - Cost-effective high-volume sending
  - Resend - Modern developer-first platform
  - SendGrid - Advanced analytics and deliverability
  - Brevo - All-in-one marketing platform
</Card>

### E-commerce Integrations

Pull product data, customer information, and order details directly from your e-commerce platform.

<Card title="Shopify" icon="shopify" href="/integrations/shopify">
  **Live product integration**
  - Select products visually from your catalog
  - Auto-pull images, prices, and descriptions
  - Customer data synchronization
  - Transactional email automation
</Card>

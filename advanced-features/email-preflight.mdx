## What Gets Checked

Preflight runs 4 comprehensive checks:

### 1. Email Client Compatibility

**Tests your email on real devices:**
- Gmail (Desktop, iOS, Android)
- Outlook (Windows, Mac, Web)
- Apple Mail (macOS, iOS)
- Yahoo Mail
- 30+ more email clients

- Images not loading
- Broken layouts
- Missing fonts
- CTA buttons not visible
- Dark mode rendering issues

### 2. Link Validation

**Checks every link in your email:**
- URL accessibility
- Response time
- HTTP status codes
- Broken/unreachable links

**Results show:**
- Working links count

- Response time for each link

### 3. Grammar & Content Quality

**Analyzes your email content:**
- Spelling errors with suggestions

- Professional quality checks

- Sender identification clear
- CAN-SPAM compliance

- Deliverability score (0-100%)

## Deliverability Scoring

**How scores are calculated:**

| Score | Placement | What It Means |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| **90-100%** | Inbox | Excellent - Will reach primary inbox |
| **80-89%** | Inbox/Promotions | Good - Should reach inbox or promotions tab |
| **60-79%** | Promotions | Fair - Likely promotions tab, some spam risk |
| **Below 60%** | Spam | Poor - High risk of spam folder |

**Factors affecting score:**
- Spam trigger words in content
- Link-to-text ratio
- Image-to-text ratio
- Sender reputation
- Authentication setup (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- Previous engagement rates
- Complaint rates

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Preflight not running" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    **If preflight doesn't start:**
    - Ensure email has content (not blank)
    - Check internet connection
    - Try refreshing the page
    - Clear browser cache
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="False positives" icon="question">
    **If you disagree with results:**
    - Review detailed breakdown
    - Some "issues" may be intentional design choices
    - You can always "Export Anyway" to proceed
    - Warnings (yellow) are suggestions, not blockers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Fix with AI not working" icon="robot">
    **If AI fix fails:**
    - Check that you have AI generations remaining
    - Ensure React Email template is editable
    - Try fixing issues manually in visual editor
    - Some issues may require manual review
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Slow preflight checks" icon="clock">
    **If checks take too long:**
    - Large images slow down analysis
    - Many links increase check time
    - Complex layouts take longer to validate
    - Wait 30-60 seconds before timing out
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Generate Images

There are two ways to generate AI images in Migma:

<iframe width="100%" height="300" src="https://youtube.com/updatelater" title="Generating AI Images" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

### Method 1: Via Creative Studio (Visual Editor)

**Access Creative Studio inside the visual editor:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Visual Editor">
    Open any email in the visual editor
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on Image">
    Click on any image placeholder or existing image
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Creative Studio">
    The **Creative Studio** dialog opens automatically
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Generate Tab">
    Click the **"Generate"** tab (with wand icon)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe Your Image">
    Enter a prompt describing the image you want:
    ```text
    Professional product photo of laptop on desk
    Bright, airy office setting
    Use our brand teal color as accent
    Include our logo in corner
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **"Generate"** and wait 5-10 seconds
  </Step>

  <Step title="Insert Image">
    Click the generated image to insert it into your email
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: Within Email Creation Prompt

**While creating an email with AI:**

```text
Create a product launch email for our new smartwatch

Generate hero image showing:
- Smartwatch on wrist
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Our brand colors (purple and gold)
- Include our logo subtly in corner
```

Migma generates both email AND custom hero image in one step.

**Then refine in Creative Studio:**

If you want to adjust the generated image:
1. Open the email in visual editor
2. Click the image
3. Creative Studio opens - go to Generate tab
4. Create a new variation with a different prompt

---

### Additional Features in Creative Studio

Generate images separately:

1. Go to **Images** page
2. Click **"Generate with AI"**
3. Describe your image
4. Generate and download
5. Use in any email

## Image Prompting Guide

### Anatomy of Good Image Prompts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subject">
    What is the main focus?
    ```text
    Professional businesswoman using laptop
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Setting/Context">
    Where/when is this?
    ```text
    in modern office with large windows
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Style/Aesthetic">
    What's the visual feel?
    ```text
    bright, clean, minimalist aesthetic
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Colors">
    What colors should dominate?
    ```text
    using our brand purple and gold accents
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Composition">
    How should it be framed?
    ```text
    centered composition, shallow depth of field
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Complete prompt:**
```text
Professional businesswoman using laptop in modern office with large
windows, bright, clean, minimalist aesthetic, using our brand purple
and gold accents, centered composition, shallow depth of field
```

## On-Brand Features

### Automatic Brand Integration

When you generate images, Migma automatically:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Applies Brand Colors" icon="palette">
    **How it works:**
    - Reads your project's color palette
    - Incorporates primary and secondary colors
    - Balances color prominence
    - Creates cohesive look

    **No need to specify** - happens automatically

    **Override:** Can request specific colors in prompt
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Includes Logo" icon="image-portrait">
    **Options:**
    - Subtle watermark style
    - Corner placement
    - Integrated into composition
    - Custom positioning

    **Example prompt:**
    ```text
    Include our logo in bottom right corner, subtle watermark
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Matches Visual Style" icon="paintbrush">
    **Learns from:**
    - Your brand's style guidelines
    - Previously uploaded images
    - Visual aesthetic preferences

    **Result:** Consistent look across all generated images
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Incorporates Products" icon="box-open">
    **Can include:**
    - Product images from your library
    - Screenshots of your app/software
    - Physical products in scenes

    **Example:**
    ```text
    Show our red sneakers being worn while jogging in park
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical Specifications

### Image Formats Generated

- **Primary:** WebP (modern, compressed)
- **Fallback:** JPG/PNG (universal compatibility)
- **Format selection:** Automatic based on email client

### Image Sizes

**Email-optimized dimensions:**
- Hero images: 1200x600px (2:1 ratio)
- Product images: 800x800px (1:1 ratio)
- Banner images: 1200x300px (4:1 ratio)
- Thumbnail: 400x400px
- Icon: 200x200px

**Automatic optimization:**
- Compressed for fast loading
- Retina-ready (2x resolution)
- Mobile-responsive variants

### CDN Integration

**All generated images:**
✅ Uploaded to global CDN
✅ Compressed automatically
✅ Delivered from nearest server
✅ Cached for performance
✅ SSL/HTTPS by default

**Result:** Fast-loading emails worldwide

## Advanced Techniques

### Multi-Image Generation

Generate entire image sets:

```text
Create 5 lifestyle images for email campaign:
1. Hero: Team collaboration in office
2. Feature 1: Close-up of dashboard UI
3. Feature 2: Mobile app in use
4. Testimonial: Happy customer headshot
5. CTA: Product on desk with coffee

All with consistent style and our brand colors
```

### Seasonal Variations

Generate seasonal versions:

```text
Create holiday version of our hero image
Add festive elements (lights, decorations)
Keep professional tone
Maintain brand colors with holiday accents
```

### A/B Testing Visuals

Generate variants:

```text
Create 3 variations of product hero image:
Variation A: Lifestyle setting
Variation B: Studio white background
Variation C: In-use demonstration

Same product, different contexts
```

  <Accordion title="Be Specific" icon="bullseye">
    **Generic:**
    "Create an image for our email"

    **Specific:**
    "Create hero image showing professional using our CRM dashboard on laptop, modern office, bright and clean, our brand blue prominent"

    Specificity = better results

  <Accordion title="Reference Your Brand" icon="bookmark">

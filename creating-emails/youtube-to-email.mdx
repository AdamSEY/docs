## Overview

Migma can watch YouTube videos for you. Paste any YouTube URL and Migma will watch the video, extract key content, and generate a ready-to-send email summary or announcement.

<Note>
**Note:** This feature is available via API and chat prompts. Simply paste a YouTube URL in your prompt and describe what kind of email you want to create from the video content.
</Note>

## How to Use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get YouTube URL">
    Copy the URL of any public YouTube video
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste in Create">
    Go to Create page and use this prompt:
    ```text
    Watch this YouTube video and create an email summary:
    https://youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID

    Include:
    - Main talking points
    - Key takeaways
    - Call to action to watch full video
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Migma Watches">
    Migma transcribes and analyzes the video (30-90 seconds)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Email">
    Generated email includes:
    - Video summary
    - Key points
    - Quotes or highlights
    - Video embed
    - CTA to watch
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize & Send">
    Edit as needed and export/send
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Good Quality Videos" icon="video">
    - Clear audio for accurate transcription
    - Well-structured content
    - Defined talking points

    Better transcription = better email content
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Specify Email Structure" icon="sitemap">
    Tell Migma how to organize the content:
    ```text
    Format as:
    1. Brief intro
    2. 3 main takeaways
    3. Supporting details
    4. CTA
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Include Context" icon="circle-info">
    Help Migma understand your goal:
    ```text
    This is a webinar recap for attendees who want
    to review key points and share with their teams
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Embed vs Link" icon="link">
    Choose your preference:
    ```text
    Include embedded video player in email
    ```
    OR
    ```text
    Add thumbnail with play button linking to YouTube
    ```
  </Accordion>

## Advanced Features

### Timestamp Highlights

Extract specific moments:
```text
Create email highlighting these moments from video:
https://youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO

Focus on:
- Product announcement (around 2:30)
- Demo walkthrough (5:00-8:00)
- Q&A insights (12:00+)
```

### Multi-Video Roundup

Summarize multiple videos:
```text
Create newsletter roundup from these 3 videos:
1. https://youtube.com/watch?v=VID1
2. https://youtube.com/watch?v=VID2
3. https://youtube.com/watch?v=VID3

Brief summary of each with links
```

### Video Series Email

Promote series:
```text
Create email promoting our video series:
Latest episode: https://youtube.com/watch?v=EP5

Include:
- This episode's highlights
- Why viewers should subscribe
- Links to previous episodes
- Tease next episode
```

## Limitations

<Warning>
**Video Requirements:**
- Must be public YouTube video
- Under 2 hours recommended
- Clear audio quality
- English language (other languages coming soon)
</Warning>

<Info>
**What Works Best:**
- Structured presentations
- Interviews and talks
- Product demos
- Educational content

**May Need Editing:**
- Highly technical jargon
- Very long videos (90+ min)
- Poor audio quality
- Multiple speakers without clear identification
</Info>

## How Remix Works

### The Remix Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Trending Emails">
    Go to **[migma.ai/trending](https://migma.ai/trending)**

    Scroll through the masonry grid and find an email you like
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hover Over Email">
    **Hover to see details:**
    - Email subject and preview
    - Tags and statistics
    - Action buttons appear

    **Options:**
    - Click **Preview** to see full email
    - Click **Remix** to use as reference
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Remix">
    Click the **"Remix"** button

    **What happens:**
    - Email reference is saved temporarily
    - You're redirected to `/create` page
    - Remix reference appears at top of create page
  </Step>

  <Step title="See Remix Reference">
    **On the create page, you'll see:**

    ```
    [Remix: "Email Subject Here"] [View Original] [√ó]
    ```

    **This chip shows:**
    - The email you're remixing from
    - Link to view the original email
    - Option to remove reference (√ó button)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your Instructions">
    **Write your prompt with modifications:**

    ```
    Create a product launch email for our new feature.

    Product: TaskMaster Pro
    Key benefits:
    - AI-powered task automation
    - Team collaboration tools
    - Analytics dashboard

    CTA: Start Free Trial
    ```

    **Migma will:**
    - Use the remix email's structure as reference
    - Apply YOUR brand colors, fonts, and logo
    - Generate content based on YOUR prompt
    - Maintain the design approach from reference
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **"Generate"**

    **AI creates email that:**
    - Follows similar structure to remix reference
    - Uses your brand identity throughout
    - Contains your specific content
    - Adapts the design to your needs
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit & Finalize">
    Review the generated email

    - Edit in visual editor if needed
    - Adjust content, images, CTAs
    - Export or send when ready
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Advanced Remix Techniques

### Combine Multiple References

**Use multiple emails for inspiration:**

While you can only remix from one email at a time in the UI, you can describe multiple references in your prompt:

```
Use the layout from this remix reference,
but add a product showcase section similar to our last newsletter,
and use the CTA style from our welcome email.
```

### Selective Remixing

**Specify what to keep:**

```
Keep the exact header layout from this remix,
but redesign the body content sections to be more minimal.
Use different imagery but maintain the same image sizes.
```

### Cross-Category Remixing

**Adapt email types:**

```
This is a newsletter remix reference.
Convert it to a product launch email by:
- Replacing news sections with product features
- Changing CTA from "Read More" to "Start Free Trial"
- Adding pricing section before footer
```

## Understanding the Technology

### How Remix Data is Stored

**When you click "Remix":**

1. **Reference data saved** to localStorage:
   ```json
   {
     "id": "email_abc123",
     "subject": "Summer Sale - 50% Off Everything",
     "previewText": "Limited time offer on all products..."
   }
   ```

2. **Redirect to create page** automatically

3. **Create page detects** the pending remix reference

4. **Shows remix chip** at top with subject and link to original

5. **When you generate**, sends `remixReferenceId` to AI backend

6. **AI uses original email** as structural/design reference

### What the AI Sees

**When you remix, the AI receives:**
- Structure and layout of original email
- Design patterns and visual hierarchy
- Section organization
- Image placement approach
- CTA positioning

**AI does NOT copy:**
- Exact text (uses your prompt)
- Brand colors (uses yours)
- Logo (uses yours)
- Fonts (uses yours)

**Result:** Your brand + proven structure = winning combination

## Removing Remix Reference

**If you change your mind:**

1. **Click √ó button** on remix chip at top of create page
2. Reference is removed
3. Start fresh or choose different remix source

**Or:**

1. **Just start typing** your prompt without worrying about it
2. Generate will still use reference if present
3. Remove later if needed

## Creation Methods

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Prompt-Based" icon="keyboard" href="/creating-emails/prompt-based-creation">
    **Fastest method** - Describe what you need in plain language

    Best for: Newsletters, promotions, announcements

    Example: "Create a summer sale email with 30% off"
  </Card>

  <Card title="Image Upload" icon="image" href="/creating-emails/image-clone">
    **From visuals** - Upload screenshots or designs to recreate

    Best for: Recreating existing designs, visual inspiration

    Formats: JPG, PNG, WebP (up to 10MB)
  </Card>

  <Card title="Figma Import" icon="figma" href="/creating-emails/figma-import">
    **Design handoff** - Import directly from Figma files

    Best for: Design teams, precise implementations

    Supports: Frames, components, Auto Layout
  </Card>

  <Card title="YouTube to Email" icon="youtube" href="/creating-emails/youtube-to-email">
    **Content repurposing** - Convert YouTube videos to emails

    Best for: Video content promotion, summaries

    Auto-extracts: Transcript, thumbnails, metadata
  </Card>

  <Card title="Email Remix" icon="clone" href="/creating-emails/remix-emails">
    **Build on existing** - Start from templates or previous emails

    Best for: Variations, seasonal updates, A/B testing

    Access: Your emails, trending templates
  </Card>

  <Card title="Multi-Language" icon="globe" href="/creating-emails/multi-language">
    **Global reach** - Generate in 15+ languages simultaneously

    Best for: International campaigns, localization

    Maintains: Brand identity across all languages
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Which method is fastest?">
    **Prompt-Based** and **Email Remix** are the fastest. You can generate an email in under 30 seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which method gives the best quality?">
    **Figma Import** gives the most precise results. **Claude 3.5 Sonnet** model gives the highest quality AI generation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I combine methods?">
    Yes! Start with one method (e.g., Image Upload) then refine with prompts. All methods lead to the same editor.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need design skills?">
    No! Prompt-based creation requires zero design skills. Just describe what you want.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I edit after generation?">
    Absolutely! Refine through chat, use the visual editor, or make manual HTML edits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

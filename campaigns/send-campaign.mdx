## Sending a Campaign

Once you've created your email and selected your audience, you're ready to send your campaign.

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Create or Select Your Email

Navigate to your email in the Email Editor or create a new one using Migma's AI tools.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Email">
    Select an existing email from your templates or create a new one
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Content">
    Make sure all content, links, and images are correct
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run Email Preflight">
    Use Email Preflight to check for spam triggers and rendering issues
  </Step>
</Steps>

### 2. Select Your Audience

Choose who will receive your campaign.

<CodeGroup>
```javascript Single List
{
  "campaign": {
    "audienceType": "list",
    "listId": "your-list-id"
  }
}
```

```javascript Multiple Lists
{
  "campaign": {
    "audienceType": "multiple",
    "listIds": ["list-1", "list-2", "list-3"]
  }
}
```

```javascript Segment
{
  "campaign": {
    "audienceType": "segment",
    "segmentId": "your-segment-id",
    "filters": {
      "openedLast30Days": true,
      "tags": ["engaged"]
    }
  }
}
```
</CodeGroup>

### 3. Configure Campaign Settings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Subject Line">
    Write a compelling subject line that encourages opens. Keep it under 50 characters for mobile optimization.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Sender Information">
    Configure your "From" name and email address. Use a recognizable sender name to increase trust.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reply-To Address">
    Set where replies will be sent. This can be different from your sending address.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Tracking Settings">
    Enable open tracking, click tracking, and conversion tracking as needed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### 4. Send Test Emails

<Warning>
  Always send test emails before launching a campaign to verify everything looks correct.
</Warning>

Send test emails to yourself and team members to verify:
- Content displays correctly
- Links work properly
- Images load correctly
- Personalization tokens populate
- Mobile rendering looks good

### 5. Schedule or Send

You have two options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Send Now">
    Launch your campaign immediately. The email will be sent to your audience within minutes.

    <Info>
      Large campaigns (10,000+ recipients) may take longer to send completely.
    </Info>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Schedule">
    Schedule your campaign for a specific date and time.

    **Benefits:**
    - Send at optimal times for your audience
    - Plan campaigns in advance
    - Coordinate with other marketing activities

    **Tip:** Schedule for Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM in your audience's timezone for best results.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Campaign Stuck in 'Sending' Status">
    This is normal for large campaigns. Check back in 15-30 minutes. If still stuck after an hour, contact support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Low Delivery Rate">
    Check your sender reputation and ensure you're following email best practices. Use Email Preflight to identify potential issues.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Recipients Not Receiving Emails">
    Verify your email service provider is properly configured and that recipient emails are valid in your contact list.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

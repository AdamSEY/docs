## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Create">
    Click **Create** in the navigation or visit [migma.ai/create](https://migma.ai/create)

    <Tip>Press `/` from anywhere to focus the prompt field instantly</Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Your Project">
    Choose which brand/project to use (includes colors, logos, voice, and guidelines)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write Your Prompt">
    Describe the email you want to create in natural language

    **Example:**
    ```
    Create a summer sale email with:
    - 30% off all products
    - Sale ends July 31st
    - Feature our new collection
    - Urgent but friendly tone
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate** or press `Ctrl/Cmd + Enter`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Refine">
    Make changes through chat: "Make the CTA button green" or "Add a testimonial section"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export">
    Download HTML, copy to clipboard, or export to Mailchimp/Klaviyo
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example Prompts

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Newsletter">
    ```
    Create a monthly newsletter announcing our new features:
    - AI-powered analytics dashboard
    - Team collaboration tools
    - Mobile app updates
    Include hero image and clear CTAs
    ```
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Promotional">
    ```
    Announce our new AI email tool launching next week:
    - 3 key benefits with icons
    - Product screenshot
    - Early bird pricing (50% off)
    - Professional, exciting tone
    ```
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Welcome">
    ```
    Create a welcome email for new subscribers:
    - Thank them for joining
    - Introduce our brand story
    - Link to getting started guide
    - Warm and friendly tone
    ```
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Event">
    ```
    Invite to our SEO masterclass webinar:
    - Date: March 15th, 2pm EST
    - Speaker: Sarah Johnson, SEO Expert
    - What they'll learn (3 points)
    - Registration CTA
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
**Pro Tips for Better Prompts:**
- Specify tone (professional, friendly, urgent, casual)
- Include structure (hero image, sections, CTAs)
- Mention specific content
- Reference target audience
- Indicate desired length
</Note>

## Refining Your Email

Make changes through conversation after generation:

<CodeGroup>
```text Change Colors
Make the CTA button green instead of purple
```

```text Adjust Copy
Make the headline more urgent and compelling
```

```text Add Section
Add a testimonial section with a customer quote
```

```text Change Layout
Move the hero image to the right side
```

```text Shorter Version
Make this more concise, reduce to 3 paragraphs
```
</CodeGroup>

<Tip>
Each message creates a new version while preserving history. You can always revert to previous versions.
</Tip>

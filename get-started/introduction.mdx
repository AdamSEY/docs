## What is Migma?

Migma is an AI-powered email creation platform that generates pixel-perfect, on-brand emails in seconds. Using advanced AI models and your brand identity, Migma creates professional emails that match your style, voice, and visual guidelines automatically.

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Creation

Multiple state-of-the-art AI models (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) work together to understand your requirements and generate on-brand emails.

- Choose from multiple AI models
- Support for 15+ languages
- Automatic subject lines and preview text
- Learns from your feedback

### Brand Memory

Once imported, Migma remembers your brand identity across all emails:

- **Visual Identity:** Logos, colors, typography, custom CSS
- **Content Voice:** Tone, personality, style guidelines
- **Templates:** Saved layouts and designs
- **Knowledge Base:** Product info, company details

### Multi-Modal Creation

Create emails from various sources:

- **Text Prompts** - Natural language descriptions
- **Images** - Screenshots or inspiration photos
- **Figma** - Import designs directly
- **HTML** - Paste existing code
- **Previous Emails** - Remix past creations

Every email is automatically optimized for:

## Understanding Projects

**Projects** (also called Brands) are containers for your brand identity. Each project stores brand assets, voice, templates, and settings.

**Use cases:**
- **Agencies:** Separate projects for each client
- **Multi-Brand Companies:** Different brand identities
- **Product Lines:** Distinct branding per product
- **Testing:** Sandbox for experimentation

<Info>
**Free Plan:** 1-3 projects | **Basic Plan:** 5 projects | **Premium Plan:** Unlimited
</Info>

## Security Settings

### Change Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Current Password">
    Enter your current password for verification
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Password">
    Create a strong new password (min. 8 characters)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Re-enter your new password to confirm
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update">
    Save changes - you'll be logged out and need to sign in again
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Enable 2FA">
    1. Navigate to Security Settings
    2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
    3. Scan QR code with authenticator app
    4. Enter verification code
    5. Save backup codes in a secure location

    **Recommended authenticator apps:**
    - Google Authenticator
    - Authy
    - 1Password
    - Microsoft Authenticator
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Backup Codes">
    When enabling 2FA, you'll receive 10 backup codes.

    <Warning>
      Save these codes in a secure location. You'll need them if you lose access to your authenticator app.
    </Warning>

    **Each code can be used once** to sign in without your authenticator app.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Active Sessions

View and manage devices where you're currently signed in:

<AccordionGroup>

## Notification Preferences

Customize how and when you receive notifications:

### Email Notifications

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Campaign Updates">
    Receive emails about your campaign performance:
    - ✅ Campaign sent successfully
    - ✅ Campaign milestones (10% opened, 50% opened, etc.)
    - ✅ Campaign completed
    - ❌ Low engagement warnings
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Team Activity">
    Get notified about team member actions:
    - ✅ New team member joins
    - ✅ Email approved/rejected
    - ❌ Comments on your emails
    - ❌ Mentions in comments
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="System Notifications">
    Important account and system updates:
    - ✅ Security alerts
    - ✅ Billing notifications
    - ✅ Service updates
    - ✅ New features

    <Note>
      Security and billing notifications cannot be disabled.
    </Note>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Marketing">
    Promotional emails and updates:
    - ❌ Product updates
    - ❌ Tips and best practices
    - ❌ Webinar invitations
    - ❌ Special offers
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### In-App Notifications

Control notifications within the Migma app:

| Notification Type | Description | Default |
|------------------|-------------|---------|
| Campaign sent | When a campaign is sent | On |
| Campaign milestone | Performance updates | On |
| Team mentions | When someone @mentions you | On |
| Approval requests | When approval is needed | On |
| Comments | New comments on your emails | Off |

### Upgrade or Downgrade

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Upgrade">
    Get access to more features and higher limits:

    1. Navigate to Subscription Settings
    2. Click "Upgrade Plan"
    3. Select your new plan
    4. Confirm payment

    **You'll be charged:**
    - Prorated amount for current billing period
    - New rate starting next billing cycle
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Downgrade">
    Switch to a lower-tier plan:

    <Warning>
      Downgrading may result in loss of features and data. Review what will be affected before confirming.
    </Warning>

    1. Navigate to Subscription Settings
    2. Click "Change Plan"
    3. Select lower tier
    4. Review what you'll lose
    5. Confirm downgrade

    **Changes take effect** at the end of your current billing period.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Cancel">
    Cancel your subscription:

    1. Navigate to Subscription Settings
    2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
    3. Provide cancellation reason (optional)
    4. Confirm cancellation

    **After cancellation:**
    - Access continues until end of billing period
    - Account converts to Free plan
    - Data is retained for 90 days
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Payment Methods

Update your payment information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Payment Method">
    Navigate to Billing → Payment Methods
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Card Details">
    Securely enter credit card information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set as Default">
    Choose whether to make it your default payment method
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your payment information is encrypted and stored securely
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Migma uses Stripe for secure payment processing. We never store your full credit card number.
</Info>

### Creating an API Key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to API Keys">
    Go to Settings → Account → API Keys
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Key">
    Click "Generate New API Key"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Permissions">
    Choose what the key can access (read-only vs full access)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Key">
    Copy and securely store your API key - it won't be shown again!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Treat API keys like passwords. Never share them publicly or commit them to version control.
</Warning>

## Data & Privacy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Export Your Data">
    Download all your data in standard formats:
    - Emails (HTML, JSON)
    - Contacts (CSV)
    - Campaigns (CSV)
    - Analytics (CSV)

    **Processing time:** 1-24 hours depending on data volume
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Delete Account">
    Permanently delete your Migma account:

    <Warning>
      This action is irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted after 30 days.
    </Warning>

    **What gets deleted:**
    - All projects and emails
    - All contacts and audiences
    - All campaign history
    - All integrations
    - Account information

    **Before deleting:**
    - Export any data you want to keep
    - Cancel active subscriptions
    - Remove team members from projects
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Privacy Controls">
    Control how your data is used:
    - Analytics and usage data
    - Product improvement feedback
    - Marketing communications
    - Third-party data sharing (none by default)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Team Collaboration

Migma makes it easy to collaborate with your team on email campaigns. Invite team members, assign roles, and work together seamlessly.

<Info>
  Team features are available on Starter plans and above. Free plans are limited to single users.
</Info>

## Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Team Settings">
    Navigate to Settings → Team Management
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    Click "Invite Team Member" and enter their email address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Role">
    Choose their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invite">
    They'll receive an email invitation to join your team
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Team Roles & Permissions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Admin">
    **Full access** to all features and settings.

    **Can:**
    - Create, edit, and delete emails
    - Send campaigns
    - Manage audience and contacts
    - Invite/remove team members
    - Configure project settings
    - Manage billing and subscription
    - Access all integrations
    - View all analytics

    **Use for:** Project managers, account owners
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Editor">
    **Content creation and campaign management** access.

    **Can:**
    - Create and edit emails
    - Send campaigns
    - Manage audience and contacts
    - View analytics
    - Access configured integrations

    **Cannot:**
    - Invite/remove team members
    - Change project settings
    - Manage billing
    - Remove integrations

    **Use for:** Content creators, email marketers
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Viewer">
    **Read-only access** to view content and analytics.

    **Can:**
    - View emails and templates
    - View campaigns
    - View analytics and reports
    - Export data

    **Cannot:**
    - Create or edit content
    - Send campaigns
    - Manage audiences
    - Change any settings

    **Use for:** Stakeholders, clients, reviewers
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Change Member Roles

<Warning>
  Changing a member's role will immediately update their permissions. They may lose access to certain features.
</Warning>

1. Navigate to Team Management
2. Click on the team member
3. Select new role from dropdown
4. Confirm the change

### Remove Team Members

Only Admins can remove team members from projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Member">
    Find the team member in the team list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Access">
    Click "Remove" next to their name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm removal - they'll immediately lose access
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Removed team members will no longer have access to the project but can be re-invited later if needed.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use Appropriate Roles">
    Assign the minimum permissions needed for each team member's responsibilities. This reduces risk and keeps workflows clear.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Regular Access Reviews">
    Periodically review team access and remove members who no longer need access to keep your team secure.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Clear Naming Conventions">
    Establish clear naming conventions for emails, campaigns, and projects so team members can easily find what they need.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use Comments for Feedback">
    Keep all feedback and discussions within Migma instead of external tools for better context and history.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

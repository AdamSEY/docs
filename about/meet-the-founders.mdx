## Background

### Previous Success: PaidTabs

Before Migma, the brothers founded **PaidTabs**, a platform serving thousands of musicians worldwide:

- **$1M ARR** (bootstrapped)
- Scaled globally without external funding
- Featured on **SVT** (Swedish National Television)

### Entrepreneurship Programs

**STING** - Selected for Sweden's leading startup accelerator program

**EWOR** - European entrepreneurship network

**Swedish Entrepreneurs** - Active in the Scandinavian startup ecosystem

## Why Migma?

After experiencing the frustrations of traditional email marketing tools, they built Migma to make email creation:

- **Fast** - Create emails in seconds with AI
- **Easy** - No coding or design skills needed
- **Smart** - AI that learns your brand
- **Affordable** - Fair, transparent pricing
